Help & Instructions

Use this chat interface to report grievances related to roads or transportation:

  1. Describe your issue in the chat
  2. Provide location details when prompted
  3. Attach photos if available
  4. Submit your grievance through the chat

For a more accessible interface with voice recording capabilities, click "Switch to Accessible Interface".
